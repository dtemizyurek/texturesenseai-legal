Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 26, 2025

1. Introduction

TextureSenseAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

By using TextureSenseAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous User ID: We assign a unique anonymous identifier through Firebase Authentication for app functionality and analytics.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect data about how you use the app via Firebase Analytics, including:
    • Features used (texture generation, AR preview, etc.)
    • Session duration and frequency
    • App performance metrics
    • User interactions and navigation patterns
  • Crash Reports: We automatically collect crash data via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability, including:
    • Stack traces and error logs
    • Device model and iOS version
    • App version and build number
    • Custom context (e.g., last action before crash)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.

2.2 User-Provided Information

  • Subscription Data: Payment and subscription information managed by Adapty and Apple App Store (we do not store credit card information).
  • Generated Content: Text prompts and generated texture images are processed by our AI services but are not permanently stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them to your account.
  • Photos: If you use the "Apply to Photo" feature, the selected photo is temporarily processed and not stored on our servers.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

We do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact support)
  • Your precise location data
  • Your contacts or calendar
  • Your camera roll (except photos you explicitly select)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To operate and maintain the app, process texture generation requests, and manage subscriptions.
  • Improve Quality: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and enhance app performance and features.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and make data-driven improvements.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Functions
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, usage data, crash reports, device info
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 Adapty

  • Purpose: Subscription management and analytics
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, subscription status, purchase events
  • Privacy Policy: adapty.io/privacy

4.3 FAL AI

  • Purpose: AI texture generation
  • Data Shared: Text prompts, input images (temporarily processed, not stored)
  • Privacy Policy: fal.ai/privacy

4.4 Google Gemini

  • Purpose: AI-powered "Apply to Photo" and "Magic Prompt" features
  • Data Shared: Text prompts, selected photos (temporarily processed, not stored)
  • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.5 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: Payment processing and subscription management
  • Data Shared: Purchase information (managed by Apple)
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party services listed above to operate the app.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified).
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure API authentication and authorization
  • Server-side rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Firebase security rules to protect user data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 14 months (Firebase default)
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days
  • User Account: Retained until you delete the app or request deletion
  • Generated Content: Stored locally on your device; we do not retain server copies

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Correction

You can request access to or correction of your personal information by contacting us.

8.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Contacting us at privacy@texturesenseai.com

8.3 Opt-Out of Analytics

You can limit analytics tracking by:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements → Disable "Share iPhone Analytics"
  • Enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in iOS Settings

8.4 Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing emails. All communications are through the App Store.

9. Children's Privacy

TextureSenseAI is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. By using the app, you consent to such transfers. We comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for EU users.
