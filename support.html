Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

If you've previously purchased Pro but don't see your benefits, go to the Paywall screen (try tapping any Pro feature) and tap the "Restore Purchase" button at the bottom.

What are the daily limits?

Free users have a daily limit on texture generations and "Apply to Photo" actions. These limits reset every day at midnight. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited access.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. You can cancel anytime by going to your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > TextureSense AI.
